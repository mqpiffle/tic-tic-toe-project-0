Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a game that pits two players against each other to see who can gain dominance over the game board.

Starting with a randomly determined first player (player X), the players alternate adding their mark to a square on the game board. The game completes when either one player wins by marking three squares consecutively(either row, column, or diagonal) or the game has come to a draw state where neither side can win.
